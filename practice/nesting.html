NESTING

BOLD TAG:

Bold tag is used to draw attention to a specific line or word.

Example: My name is <b>Deepak Prajapati</b>
My name is Deepak Prajapati

STRONG TAG:

Strong tag is same as bold tag.
For SEO purpose.

Example: I want to become a <strong> front-end developer</strong>
I want to become a front-end developer

ITALLIC TAG:

Itallic tag is used to give itallic font to the text.

Example: I am from <i>Umbergoan</i>
I am from Umbergoan

EMPHASIZED TAG:

Emphasized tag is same as itallic tag.

Example: I love to ride <em>bikes </em>
I love to ride bikes

DELETE TAG:

delete tag is to get a line on a text

Example: I <del>dont'</del> want to play PUBG.
I don't want to play PUBG.

INSERT TAG:

Insert tag is used to highlight text by giving underline.

Example: I have played <ins>state level volleyball competition</ins>.
I have played state level volleyball competition.

UNDERLINE TAG:

Underline tag is same as insert tag.

Example: <u>Gyming</u> is my favourite activity.
Gyming is my favourite activity

STRIKE TAG:

Strike tag same as delete tag.
Strike tag is now discontinued.

Example: I love to spend time at <strike>beach</strike>.
I love to spend time at beach.

BIG TAG:

Big tag is used to get the text bigger than the normal text

Example: I love my <big>parents</big>.
I love my parents.

SMALL TAG

Small tag is used to get the text smaller than the normal text

Example: I have completed my college from <small>rofel</small>
I have completed my college from rofel

SUBSCRIPTED TAG

Subscripted tag is mainly use for chemical properties.

Example: I drink 5 litre H <sub>2</sub>o daily.
I drink 5 litre H 2O daily.

SUPERSCRIPTED TAG:

This tag is mainly use for mathematics and other purpose.

Example: My birthday is on 27<sup>th</sup> November.
My birthdayis on 27 th November.
